The Shetland Sheepdog is a small, active, and agile herding dog standing between 13 and 16 inches at the shoulder. The long coat is harsh and straight, with a dense undercoat, and comes in black, blue merle, and sable, with white markings. Shelties (and their owners) enjoy the challenge of coming up with new things to learn, from “helping” around the house to canine gymnastics. They're considered extremely intelligent and respond well if you're patient and also make training time fun. Often, the Sheltie is timid and reserved towards strangers, and it will let its voice be heard on this when need be. Although the tendency to bark a lot is considered by some to be a fault, it is this characteristic that makes the Sheltie an excellent watch dog.

Game development

My first experience with game development took place in 2019, when I started learning Unity and C# to make a game about a little pink horse jumping around. However, I did not have enough knowledge and was not quite capable of making a game yet.

"Catch 'em all" is a funny little game, which was made as a joke for friends in a few days, basically during one of my free weekends. It has "adult content", cartoonish 18+ images and inappropriate sounds. Just a litle game to have a laugh, when you are in the right childish mood.

Then in 2022 came the first "success" - the "Catch 'em all" game, which at this moment (03.2025) has over 13k views and over 600 downloads on itch.io.
